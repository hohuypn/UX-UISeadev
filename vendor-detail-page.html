Our Vision

Skygear Solutions maintains the outlook that UAS "drones" offer amazing positive benefits to society both in the commercial realm and for recreation. The more education that the public receives, the better perception our industry receives.

About Us

Skygear has a dedication to the promotion of safe use of Unmanned Aircraft Systems. We put this dedication into motion through active training, speaking to the public, and social media engagement. Sales of our products are also always accompanied by literature and resources for safe and responsible flying.
